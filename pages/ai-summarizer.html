🔑 Setup (one-time, takes 30 seconds)

This uses Google Gemini AI (completely free). Just grab an API key:

  1. Go to aistudio.google.com/apikey
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Click "Create API Key" and copy it
  4. Paste it below and you're done!

💡 How to Use

  • Paste your notes (any length) in the left box
  • Choose a mode: Summary, Key Points, or Q&A
  • Click "Summarize" and get instant AI-powered results
  • Copy the output and save it to your notes!
